## 📌 1. Project Overview

This end-to-end marketing analytics project evaluates the effectiveness of a **Gold Membership** promotional campaign run by a retail superstore chain. Using customer demographics, purchasing behavior, campaign interactions, and spending patterns, the analysis identifies high-value customer segments, measures campaign performance, and delivers data-driven recommendations to improve future membership conversions and retention.

## 💡 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) & Insights

<details open>
<summary><strong>📈 A. Campaign Performance Summary</strong></summary>
<br/>

| KPI | Performance |
| :--- | :--- |
| Total Customers | 2,240 |
| Membership Acceptance Rate | 14.91% |
| Average Customer Income | $51.69K |
| Total Customer Revenue | $1.36M |
| Best Performing Channel | Store Purchases (38.96%) |
| Average Recency (Accepted Customers) | 35 Days |

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>👥 B. Customer Segmentation & Behavioral Insights</strong></summary>
<br/>

- **Senior customers (70+ years)** generate the highest average spending levels, indicating strong purchasing power despite relatively lower digital engagement.
- **Widow customer segments** demonstrate the highest Gold Membership acceptance rates — a valuable target audience for future campaigns.
- Customers who accepted the Gold Membership offer generated roughly **36% higher web purchases** than non-responders.
- Customers with recent purchase activity show significantly higher conversion potential than dormant customers.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🍷 C. Product & Revenue Analysis</strong></summary>
<br/>

| Evaluation Category | Top Performing Segment |
| :--- | :--- |
| 🏆 Highest Spending Product Category | Wine Products |
| 🥩 Second Highest Spending Category | Meat Products |
| 🌐 Best Digital Conversion Segment | Web Purchasers |
| 💰 Highest Value Customer Group | High-Income Customers |

> **Key Observation:** Wine and Meat products collectively contribute roughly **85% of total product spending**, making premium-product customers the most valuable membership prospects.

</details>

## 🎯 5. Statistical Modeling

- **Hypothesis Testing:** Run to mathematically prove the spending behavioral differences between campaign responders and non-responders.
- **Predictive Model:** Built a **Logistic Regression model** using *Income*, *Recency*, and *Web Purchases* to predict which customers are most likely to accept a membership offer next.

## 🚀 6. Business Recommendations

| Recommendation | Objective |
|---|---|
| 🎯 Target High-Income Digital Customers | Increase membership conversions through personalized digital campaigns |
| 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Introduce Family Membership Plans | Improve engagement among households with children |
| 🔄 Re-Engage Dormant Customers | Reactivate inactive customers using targeted offers and discounts |
| 💎 Upsell Premium Product Buyers | Promote Gold Membership to high-spending Wine & Meat customers |
| 📊 Optimize Customer Segmentation | Use predictive analytics to focus marketing efforts on high-conversion prospects |
