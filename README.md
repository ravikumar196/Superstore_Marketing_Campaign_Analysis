# 🛒 Superstore Gold Membership — Campaign Analysis

> End-to-end data analytics capstone project analysing customer behaviour
> to improve Gold Membership uptake for a retail superstore chain.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The Superstore runs a year-end campaign offering customers a **20% discount Gold Membership**.
This project analyses ~2,200 customers across 22 features to answer:
- Who is most likely to accept the membership?
- Which demographics and channels drive the highest spend?
- What strategies can improve the next campaign?

## 🔍 Key Insights

- The **70+ age group** has the highest average spend (₹1.04K) but low digital engagement
- **Widow customers** show the highest response rates — targeted campaigns could yield strong ROI
- **Accepted customers** purchase 36% more via web than non-accepted customers
- **Households with kids** have significantly lower response rates — family-tier offer needed
- **Wine & Meat** together account for ~85% of total product spend

---

## 💡 Recommendations

1. Launch targeted digital campaigns for high-income web-active customers
2. Design family-oriented membership tiers for households with children
3. Re-engage dormant customers (high recency) via catalogue & discount offers
4. Upsell Gold Membership to high wine/meat spenders — already brand-loyal
